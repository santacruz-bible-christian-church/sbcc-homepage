About Us

Our History

Founded in 1985, Santa Cruz Bible Christian Church has been a beacon of hope in our city for over three decades. We started as a small bible study group and have grown into a vibrant family of believers.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To know Christ and make Him known.

Vision: To see our city transformed by the love and power of the Gospel, one life at a time.
